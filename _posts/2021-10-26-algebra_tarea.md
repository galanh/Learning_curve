## Suma de polinomios

  ----  {factor()} ----
(x**2-x+4)-(3*x**2+x-2) = -2*(x**2 + x - 3)

  ----  {factor()} ----
(5*x**2+7*y**2+2*z)-(9*y**2-2*x**2-z) = 7*x**2 - 2*y**2 + 3*z

## Multiplicacion de polinomios

  ----  {expand()} ----
(2*x+2*y)*(4*x-5*y) = 8*x**2 - 2*x*y - 10*y**2

  ----  {expand()} ----
(2*t-1)*(t+3) = 2*t**2 + 5*t - 3

  ----  {expand()} ----
(5*y**3+2)*(y-5+y**3) = 5*y**6 + 5*y**4 - 23*y**3 + 2*y - 10
